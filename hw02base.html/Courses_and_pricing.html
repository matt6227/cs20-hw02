Extra information

Difference Between Private and Regular Lessons: For regular lessons, we will pick you up with the previous student in the car, and during your lesson as you are driving, we will drop off the previous student. Then at the end of your lesson, we will pickup the next student and then we will drive you back. Please be prepared to spend extra time in the car after your appointment ends as you are being dropped off.For private lessons, we will block out time before the lesson, so that there is no one to drop off and you will be driving alone with an instructor through the duration of your lesson. However, we will pickup next student at the end of your lesson, unless the next student is also private, and then we will drop you off.
